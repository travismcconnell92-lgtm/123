Check Your Florida Lien Deadline

Most Florida construction liens must be recorded within 90 days after the last date you furnished labor, services, or materials.

This deadline is an estimate only. Lien rights may depend on additional legal factors. Do not rely on this calculator to determine actual lien deadlines.

One Small Error Can Destroy a Construction Lien

Florida construction lien law is technical. A single mistake can make a lien unenforceable.

  • Incorrect legal property description
  • Wrong property owner name
  • Failure to include statutory language
  • Incorrect lien amount
  • Miscalculated deadlines

Many contractors only learn this after recording a lien that cannot be enforced.
